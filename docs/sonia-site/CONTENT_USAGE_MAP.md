# Sonia McRorey Crawl Usage Map

This rebuild uses `docs/sonia-site/raw-crawl/imagencoach/` as the source corpus.

## Homepage Source

- `homepage.md`: hero copy, internal/external alignment, services intro, modalities, audience segments, FAQ, latest article, vision quote, testimonials and contact details.
- `sobre-sonia-mcrorey-asesora-de-imagen.md`: Sonia positioning, AICI role, education, methodology, internal/external system language and closing philosophy.

## Service Sources

- `servicios-asesoria-de-imagen-coaching.md`: services hub, modalities, service selection FAQ and coaching de abundancia overview.
- `servicios-asesoria-de-imagen-coaching-asesoria-de-imagen.md`: asesoría integral explanation, process, scope, image aspects, shopping, coaching distinction and next step.
- `servicios-asesoria-de-imagen-coaching-coaching-de-imagen.md`: coaching de imagen definition, internal blockers, difference with asesoría, benefits and fit criteria.
- `servicios-asesoria-de-imagen-coaching-talleres.md`: talleres positioning, practical workshop model, people/groups, companies/brands and corporate image friction.
- `servicios-asesoria-de-imagen-coaching-coaching-de-abundancia.md`: abundance and personal power coaching, decision resistance, nervous system, money/value/visibility language.
- `servicios-asesoria-de-imagen-coaching-preguntas-frequentes.md`: expanded FAQ language for image, coaching, companies, money/value and professional projection.

## Editorial Sources

The homepage publication library uses the crawled `imagen-presencia-*.md` pages and groups them into four visible clusters:

- Presence, identity and leadership.
- Integral image, style and wardrobe.
- Colorimetry, face and proportions.
- Companies, workshops, mindset and abundance.

## Design Guardrails Applied

- Low contrast lavender/green palette, avoiding brown, harsh black, neon and high contrast.
- Cormorant Garamond headings with Inter body copy.
- Sonia logo and `sonia-icon.svg` used in header, hero, about, CTA and favicon.
- Source copy segmented into rows, service cards, editorial clusters and FAQ instead of long unstructured paragraphs.
- No visible bullet markers in the homepage body.
