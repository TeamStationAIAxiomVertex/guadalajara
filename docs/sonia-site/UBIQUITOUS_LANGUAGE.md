# Sonia McRorey Site Ubiquitous Language

## Purpose
This document is the semantic source of truth for the Guadalajara single page site and related service pages. It exists to stop brand drift, copy hallucination, color drift, and SEO over optimization.

The site must sound like Sonia McRorey, not like a generic marketing agency, luxury influencer, manifestation coach, personal branding guru, or spa brand.

## Core positioning
Sonia is positioned as a consultora de imagen ejecutiva and coach de presencia profesional in Guadalajara.

Her work connects:

- imagen ejecutiva
- presencia profesional
- percepción profesional
- identidad
- coherencia
- liderazgo
- decisiones
- visibilidad
- autoridad
- confianza
- posicionamiento
- responsabilidad
- claridad
- autoconcepto
- comunicación
- guardarropa estratégico
- talleres corporativos
- consultoría empresarial

The work is not superficial styling. It is the alignment of internal identity, external presence, and professional perception.

## Canonical language
Use these phrases and semantic patterns as the primary vocabulary:

- Hay momentos donde hacer bien tu trabajo ya no es suficiente.
- Porque puedes tener el nivel, pero si no se refleja, no se percibe.
- La imagen no se construye desde la imitación ni desde normas externas.
- La imagen se sostiene cuando existe coherencia entre lo que haces, lo que decides y lo que proyectas.
- Alinear identidad, presencia y decisiones.
- Sostener un nuevo nivel profesional.
- Presencia coherente con la responsabilidad que hoy sostienes.
- Imagen ejecutiva clara, elegante y coherente.
- Percepción profesional.
- Claridad, confianza y autoridad.
- Presencia profesional para líderes, profesionistas y empresas.

## Tone
The voice must be:

- calm
- intelligent
- observational
- human
- reflective
- elegant
- executive
- subtle
- grounded
- psychologically mature

The voice must not be:

- loud
- hype driven
- over sold
- generic SEO copy
- influencer copy
- luxury spa copy
- startup SaaS copy
- manifestation copy

## Sentence rules
- Keep paragraphs short.
- No paragraph should be longer than two visual lines on desktop.
- Use reflective statements.
- Avoid long promotional blocks.
- Avoid over explaining.
- Let whitespace carry authority.
- Use simple Spanish with professional elegance.

## Brand promise
The site should communicate this idea:

Sonia helps leaders, professionals, entrepreneurs, and teams align image, identity, presence, and perception so their professional level is clearly understood by others.

## SEO rule
SEO must support Sonia's voice. SEO must never rewrite Sonia's identity.

The correct standard is:

Sonia with SEO support.

Never:

SEO impersonating Sonia.
